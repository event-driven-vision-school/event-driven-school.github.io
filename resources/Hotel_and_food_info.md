# EVS School venue information

## Venue
**Hotel Punta San Martino**  
Via della Punta San Martino, 4 - 16011 Arenzano (Genova)

---

## Accommodation and Meals

The school fee includes accommodation and meals at the hotel according to the following schedule:

- **Sunday, August 17th:** lunch is **not included**.
- **Monday, August 18th – Friday, August 22nd:** breakfast, lunch, and dinner are included.
- **Saturday, August 23rd:** lunch is **not included**.

Food allergies and dietary preferences will be handled directly by the hotel. The staff should already have the information provided during registration.

Water will always be available during the event. To help reduce plastic waste, participants are encouraged to bring a reusable water bottle.

The Social Dinner will take place at [Calypso Terrazza Mare](https://calypsoterrazzamare.com/).

---


## By Car

The hotel can be reached by car.

Parking spaces are available at the hotel for an additional fee (10 euros per day). To reserve a parking spot or request more information, please contact the hotel directly:

- **Email:** info@hotelpsm.it  
- **Phone:** +39 010 91081

## By Plane

The nearest airport is **Genova Cristoforo Colombo Airport (GOA)**.

From the airport, you can reach **Genova Sestri Ponente Areoporto** railway station by internal Airport Shuttle.
From the train station, you can take a regional train to **Arenzano**.

For more info on the Airport shuttle, visit [Airport Shuttle](https://www.airport.genova.it/en/transportation-airport-2/)

---

## By Train

From **Genova Piazza Principe** station, you can take a regional train to **Arenzano**.

[Trenitalia](https://www.trenitalia.com/it.html)

From Arenzano train station, the hotel is approximately a **20-minute walk** away.

---

## By Bus

Due to the hotel's location in a private area of Arenzano, there is no local bus service directly reaching the venue.

The nearest reachable bus stop can be found here:

[Nearest bus stop](https://maps.app.goo.gl/maZBjLtMxLoypFZy6)

From there, a short uphill walk of approximately **10 minutes** is required to reach the hotel.
