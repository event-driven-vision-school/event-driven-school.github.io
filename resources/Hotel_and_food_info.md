# EVS School venue information

## Venue
**Hotel Punta San Martino**  
Via della Punta San Martino, 4 - 16011 Arenzano (Genova)

---

## Accommodation and Meals

The school fee includes meals at the hotel:

- **Students:** breakfast, lunch, and dinner are included.
- **Teaching Assistants (TAs):** only lunch is included. It is possible to participate in the social dinner scheduled for Wednesday, 20th May.

---


## By Car

The hotel can be reached by car.

Parking spaces are available at the hotel for an additional fee. To reserve a parking spot or request information about pricing, please contact the hotel directly:

- **Email:** info@hotelpsm.it  
- **Phone:** +39 010 91081

---

## By Train

From **Genova Piazza Principe** station, you can take a regional train to **Arenzano**.

[Trenitalia](https://www.trenitalia.com/it.html)

From Arenzano train station, the hotel is approximately a **20-minute walk** away.

---

## By Bus

Due to the hotel's location in a private area of Arenzano, there is no local bus service directly reaching the venue.

The nearest reachable bus stop can be found here:

[Nearest bus stop](https://maps.app.goo.gl/maZBjLtMxLoypFZy6)

From there, a short uphill walk of approximately **10 minutes** is required to reach the hotel.
