# Communications Channel

During the school, all communications will take place through the **Mattermost** "slack-like" platform provided by the `neuromorphs.net` domain.

## 1. Create Your Neuromorphs Account

First, create an account on the Neuromorphs Single Sign-On (SSO) portal:

- https://login.neuromorphs.net/

This account gives you access to all Neuromorphs services.

---

## 2. Join the Mattermost EVS Team

After creating your account, join the EVS Mattermost workspace using the following invitation link:

- https://mm.neuromorphs.net/signup_user_complete/?id=hd7teiyotjbqdj9r94gbusnzgy&md=link&sbr=su

---

## 3. Install the Mattermost App (Recommended)

Mattermost is also available as a mobile application for both Android and iOS.

Installing the app is recommended for:
- receiving announcements and updates,
- communicating with other participants,
- organizing shared transportation (e.g. airport or train station taxi sharing).
